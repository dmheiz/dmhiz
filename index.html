About Me

I'm Muhsin Ibrahim, known online as D Mhiz, a software engineering student, visionary founder, and creative leader from Kano, Nigeria.

From crafting clothes as a tailor to writing code as a software engineer, my journey is deeply rooted in using technology for real-life transformation.

I am the mind behind MemoryWeb, an AI-based platform turning oral knowledge into structured digital memory. I also founded TechRise Kano and now lead Xelorra Tech Industry.

My passion lies in blending culture, technology, and leadership to build the future Nigeria needs.

MemoryWeb

MemoryWeb is a globally relevant platform that transforms oral traditions and personal memories into structured digital data. Users can submit text or voice recordings, which are then processed using AI to generate meaningful content preserved forever. The system will eventually allow users to train a personal AI based on their memories.
